Table

There are two different types of tables (with border and without border). Below will show you examples of the tables with the same information in the table.

Example 1 (borderless):

Borderless

Example 2 (Border):

Borderless

Lists

There are three different types of list: ordered list (numbered), unordered list (bullets), and description. Below are examples of lists

Example 1 (ordered list):

  1. Dogs
  2. Cats
  3. Rabbits

Example 2 (unordered list):

  • Dogs
  • Cats
  • Rabbits

Example 3 (description):

Dogs
loyal trained
Cats
sleeps cute
Rabbits
fluffy lucky
